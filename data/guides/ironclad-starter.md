## Ironclad Overview

The [[character:Ironclad]] is the starting character in Slay the Spire 2 and the most straightforward to learn. With high base HP and the [[relic:Burning Blood]] relic that heals 6 HP after every fight, the Ironclad can afford to take some hits while building up powerful offensive strategies.

## Key Archetypes

### Strength Build

Stack [[power:Strength]] to amplify all your attacks. Cards like [[Inflame]] and [[Demon Form]] provide scaling Strength that makes even basic [[Strike]]s hit hard.

**Key cards:** [[Inflame]], [[Heavy Blade]], [[Demon Form]], [[Limit Break]]

### Exhaust Build

Use cards that [[keyword:Exhaust]] other cards for powerful effects. This archetype thins your deck mid-combat and can trigger powerful synergies with [[Feel No Pain]].

**Key cards:** [[Feel No Pain]], [[Corruption]], [[Dark Embrace]], [[Fiend Fire]]

### Block Build

Focus on generating massive amounts of [[keyword:Block]] to become nearly invincible. Works well with [[Barricade]] to [[keyword:Retain]] Block between turns.

**Key cards:** [[Barricade]], [[Entrench]], [[Body Slam]], [[Impervious]]

## Starter Deck

Your starting deck contains 5 [[Strike]]s and 4 [[Defend]]s plus [[Bash]]. [[Bash]] applies [[power:Vulnerable]] which increases damage taken by 50% — always use it before your big attacks.

## General Tips

- [[relic:Burning Blood]] heals HP after every fight — use this to be more aggressive in early fights
- [[power:Strength]] scaling is one of the most powerful strategies in the game
- Don't underestimate [[keyword:Exhaust]] synergies — they can carry entire runs
- [[Body Slam]] converts Block into damage, bridging offense and defense
- [[potion:Fire Potion]] is great for bursting down elites in Act 1
